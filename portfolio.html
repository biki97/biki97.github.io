01 / Origin story

A path that wasn't linear.

And that's exactly the point — every detour taught me how data really behaves.

I take the messy, real-world version of a thing and give it structure until it works in production.

After a B.Sc. in IT, I worked as a Data Entry Operator and Team Leader at Jorhat Medical College and Inland Water Transport — learning the discipline of handling large, structured datasets at scale.

In 2022 I went back to school, cleared JMEE, and finished an MCA from Jorhat Engineering College. During it, I interned at the National Informatics Centre, Shillong as a full-stack developer, building a tax-filing system on Spring Boot and PostgreSQL.

Most recently, I worked at Borde, Inc. as a Data Quality Engineer on their Guwahati data science team — annotating training data in Label Studio, running inference on production datasets, and turning real-world failure modes into structured improvements the team could ship.
